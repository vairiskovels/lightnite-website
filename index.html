About Us

Founded in 2021 by Vairis Kovels, Lightnite has come a long way from its beginnings in Riga. When he first started out, his passion for eco-friendly electric skateboards drove him to quit day job so that Lightnite can offer you the world's best electric skateboards. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into something bigger.
